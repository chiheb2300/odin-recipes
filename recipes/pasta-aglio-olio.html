Spaghetti aglio, olio e peperoncino| Spaghetti with garlic, oil and chili pepper

Description:

Spaghetti aglio, olio e peperoncino is a very simple and tasty dish from the italian cuisine.

This is the first recipe that comes to mind when talking about quick to make first dishes, especially when the fridge is empty. Its sucess derives from the choice of ingridients: pasta, garlic, oil and chili pepper!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 11.3oz
  • Fresh chili pepper 3
  • Fine salt to tale
  • Garlic 3 cloves
  • Extra virgin olive oil 70g

Steps

  1. To prepare the garlic, oil and chili pepper spaghetti, start by cooking the pasta in boiling salted water. Cook the spaghetti al dente.
  2. In the meantime prepare the seasoning: peel the garlic cloves, divide them in half and remove the core (the green central part of each clove).
  3. Place the pan over very low heat and add the sliced garlic and chili peppers. Continue to fry over very low heat; the chili and garlic should not burn, and should only fry for a couple of minutes.
  4. For even browning without the risk of burning, you can tilt the pan to gather the oil, garlic and chili peppers in one area and allow them to brown evenly.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked al dente, you can transfer it directly into the pan and add a ladle of cooking water.
  6. Stir for a few moments to combine the flavors and saut√©, then you can plate your garlic, oil and chili pepper spaghetti and serve very hot!
